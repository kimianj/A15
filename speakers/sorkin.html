New York Times coliminist and author, Andrew Ross Sorkin, has been described as " the most famous journalist of his generation" His book, Too Big to Fail, was the first behind-the-screen account of the finanical crisis that led to our current recession.
The Economist, The Financial Times, and Bussiness Week all named Too Big to Fail one of the best books of the year. The book was published by Vukung October 20, 2009. The book was adopted as moved by HBO Folms and premiered in HBO on May 23, 2011.
